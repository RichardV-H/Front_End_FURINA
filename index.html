Furina

The "Regina of All Waters, Kindreds, Peoples and Laws" is deeply loved by her people. She follows each and every trial held at the Opera Epiclese with an inextinguishable passion, and is always acutely aware of how the "audience" sees things.


Real name Furina de Fontaine is a playable Hydro character in Genshin Impact who can alternate between Pneuma and Ousia alignments.


Furina is introduced as the bombastic and sometimes overconfident Hydro Archon. However, this is revealed to be a public persona necessitated by the prophecy, and after the truth is exposed, she discards her guise and abdicates to live a much humbler life.
